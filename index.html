Scooter Braun

  • 2002: Scooter Braun organizing parties while studying at Emory University in Atlanta, Georga. Scooter was hired to plan after parties in each of five cities on the Anger Management Tour, featuring Ludacris and Eminem.
  • 2002: The connections Scooter made during party planning eventually led to meeting big time producer Jermaine Dupri who is the director of So So Def Records.
  • 2007: Scooter late one night was searching for talent to potentially sign to his label. A video that Youtube recommended him to watch next was a 12 year old Justin Bieber singing So Sick by Neyo.
  • 2007: Scooter gets into contact with Justin Bieber's mom and convinces her and Justin to fly out to Atlanta to meet in a no-strings attatched trial period
  • 2011: Usher's metor L.A Reid signed Beiber to a deal with Island Def Jam
